  • Horizon Zero Dawn

    Horizon Zero Dawn – VFS Final Sound Design Project
    Close
    My role on it

    This is my final Sound Design project that I have done as result of my year at the Vancouver Film School. All the rights are reserved and belong to Sony Interactive Entertainment Europe and Guerrilla Games.

    I was responsible for all the sounds replacement from the recordings, to the editing and the final mix as well. I also edited the music that I choose and was provided by 5Alarm. This one here is a Stereo Fold Down of the original 5.1 Surround mix that I have done in a Dolby certified theater.

    Close

    Hostile Inn is part of an episodic series created by some students at the Vancouver Film School.   • Shift 2: Unleashed

    Shift 2: Unleashed – Waves Test Package
    Close
    My role on it

    This was a test project with the goal of creating a piece of sound design with a limited amount of audio assets and 24 hours of time at disposal. The video is a courtesy from EA and it was originally used in a Sound Design competition organized by Waves. All the rights for the video are reserved and belongs to Electronic Arts.

    When I first approached this task I decided not to use any normal engine sounds for the cars, instead I heavily experimented with the Kyma software in creating the core of my Sound Design. Along with that I added some SFX to give some more details but I wanted to keep the whole piece sounding more unrealistic and different. All of this trying to keep the trailer interesting to watch.

    • SFX Editor
    • Sound Design
    • Stereo Mix

    My role on it

    This is a breakdown of the Wwise project that I created in order to implement the sounds in the game. Teto is the result of a collaboration that I did between the Sound Design and the Game Design campus of the Vancouver Film School.

  • Teto: Outrun the Dark

    Teto: Outrun the Dark – Gameplay
    Close
    My role on it

    Teto: Outrun the dark, is a 3D third-person puzzle platformer for PC where you play as a light spirit trying to revive a dead world back to its former state. This game is developed by some students of the Game Design program at the Vancouver Film School.

    My main role for this collaboration was to create the Sound Design, recording the Foley and the voices. I have been also part of the implementation between Wwise and the Unity Engine. I was responsible to lead a team of three other Sound Designers and I was the point of contact with the Programmers and the Game Designers. Creating the sounds for this game was fun and interesting, especially because it was something that I had never done before and it taught me a lot regarding how to better work with a team of developers.

    • Foley Artist and Recordist
    • ADR Recordist
    • Sound Editor
    • Wwise Implementation
